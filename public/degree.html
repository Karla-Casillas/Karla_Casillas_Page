Purpose

The dual degree program in Computer Engineering and Business and Data Analytics aims to equip students with a strong foundation in technology and business, combining analytical and technical skills with strategic thinking. This program prepares graduates to tackle complex challenges in the digital era.

Structure

The degree spans six years, with a comprehensive curriculum that integrates computer engineering and business analytics. Each year focuses on specific subjects designed to provide both theoretical knowledge and practical experience.
